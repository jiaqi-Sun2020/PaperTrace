# Evidence rubric

0 exposure only; 1 self-report; 2 recognition; 3 prompted recall; 4 unprompted explanation; 5 direct application; 6 transfer; 7 delayed recall/application. Prompted and unprompted evidence are distinct. Completion, HTML viewing, and source exposure are not evidence. Errors retain a misconception; uncertain evidence triggers diagnosis rather than an automatic downgrade.

The profile's existing `unrated`, `unknown`, `learning`, `known`, and `mastered` meanings are unchanged. Stronger evidence can justify a proposal, but the safe importer remains the authority for mutation.
