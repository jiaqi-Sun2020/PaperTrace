# Company Report Sources

Use this reference when the user asks to include company AI reports, safety frameworks, research blogs, model cards, or major official announcements.

## Source Priority

1. Official company report, system card, model card, safety framework, research blog, policy post, or documentation.
2. Peer-reviewed paper, arXiv preprint by company authors, or conference publication.
3. Reuters/AP/FT/WSJ/Bloomberg/Nature/Science/MIT Technology Review for independent reporting.
4. Community discussion only for sentiment and developer adoption, never as sole confirmation.

```text

- 安全或监管信号:
- 对 QWTA / CTQW / AI for Quantum 的启发:
```

## Red Flags

Do not over-trust:
- internal benchmark claims without public artifacts
- leaked roadmap names
- Reddit/X summaries claiming unreleased models
- "AGI achieved" style marketing language
- unclear date articles recirculated by aggregators

If only weak sources exist, label the item as unverified or omit it.
